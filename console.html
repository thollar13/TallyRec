Why Create A League?

Creating a league takes TallyRec to a whole new level. It allows park managers to create the teams themselves, easily enter the league schedule, and then simply have your coaches connect to their teams with their schedule already present. It allows the park manager to directly send important custom messages to all members of the park (weather changes, delays, park announcements, etc.).
